# KLE Priority Page Outlines

All outlines are conversion-first and support OC/LA search intent.

## 1) Home (`/`)

### Goal
- Clarify who KLE helps, what outcomes it drives, and route visitors into the best-fit service path.

### Recommended Section Order
1. Hero
2. Immediate trust strip
3. Service pathways
4. Outcomes snapshot
5. Method snapshot
6. Featured case studies
7. FAQ + objections
8. Final CTA

### Copy Structure
- **H1:** Outcome-led leadership transformation headline.
- **Subhead:** 1-2 sentences combining empathy and execution.
- **Primary CTA:** Book a free consultation.
- **Secondary CTA:** Take the alignment assessment.

### Must-Include Blocks
- Logo strip above the fold.
- Three high-impact stats.
- Two short testimonial quotes (one on speed, one on leadership shift).
- Pathways to:
  - `/services/executive-coaching`
  - `/services/fractional-c-suite`
  - `/services/strategy-facilitation`

## 2) Services Hub (`/services`)

### Goal
- Help visitors self-diagnose and choose the correct engagement.

### Recommended Section Order
1. Service chooser hero
2. "If this sounds like you" challenge matrix
3. Service cards with outcomes
4. Comparison table (coaching vs fractional vs facilitation)
5. Process and timeline snapshot
6. Conversion CTA

### Required UX Pattern
- Decision framework:
  - Need leadership behaviour change -> executive coaching.
  - Need embedded function-level leadership -> fractional C-suite.
  - Need strategic alignment event/cadence -> strategy facilitation.

## 3) Executive Coaching Page (`/services/executive-coaching`)

### Goal
- Convert CEOs and leadership teams who are stuck in management mode and need scale-ready leadership.

### Recommended Section Order
1. Problem-led hero
2. Who this is for / not for
3. Outcomes and measurable shifts
4. How coaching works (week 0-12)
5. Proof and case examples
6. FAQ (time, stakeholder buy-in, expected pace)
7. CTA

### Messaging Priorities
- Emphasise transition from "in the weeds" to strategic leadership.
- Highlight measurable behavioural outcomes:
  - better delegation
  - faster decisions
  - improved team ownership

## 4) Fractional C-Suite Hub (`/services/fractional-c-suite`)

### Goal
- Clarify when fractional leadership is the right model and route to role-specific pages.

### Recommended Section Order
1. Hero (when to choose fractional)
2. Ideal use cases
3. Role-based pathways (CFO/CMO/CSO/CHRO)
4. 30/60/90-day model
5. Governance and stakeholder alignment
6. Results proof
7. CTA

### Critical Content Block
- "Working with your existing leaders" section to reduce internal threat concerns.

## 5) Fractional Role Page Template (example: `/services/fractional-cfo`)

### Goal
- Capture role-specific commercial intent and convert quickly.

### Template Structure
1. **H1:** Fractional [Role] for [Audience/Outcome].
2. **Problem section:** Symptoms that indicate need.
3. **Outcome section:** Business outcomes in first 90 days.
4. **Scope section:** What KLE owns vs internal team.
5. **Proof section:** Relevant case outcomes + quote.
6. **Fit section:** Who should and should not engage.
7. **FAQ:** pricing model, time commitment, onboarding.
8. **CTA:** Book a [Role] consultation.

## 6) Approach Page (`/approach`)

### Goal
- De-risk the engagement by making process, cadence, and communication explicit.

### Recommended Section Order
1. Hero ("How we work")
2. Principles (listen-first, candid, execution-led)
3. Engagement phases (discover, align, execute, embed)
4. Cadence and communication standards
5. Measurement model (what gets tracked and when)
6. Practical deliverables/examples
7. CTA

### Required Clarity Topics
- Explain "right-sized engagement" options.
- Explain check-in rhythm and leadership visibility expectations.

## 7) Results Hub (`/results`)

### Goal
- Provide a single high-trust destination for measurable outcomes and social proof.

### Recommended Section Order
1. Hero with top-line outcomes
2. KPI cards
3. Case study highlights
4. Testimonial blocks by persona
5. "What changed" operational outcomes
6. CTA

### KPI Categories
- Leadership behaviour change
- Team performance/structure change
- Business performance acceleration

4. Local trust (HQ, travel model, nearby sectors)

6. CTA

## Outline Production Notes For Content Team

- Keep paragraphs short and scannable.
- Use more visual hierarchy and fewer dense text blocks.
- Lead each page with outcomes, then method.
- Use client voice language where possible:
  - "more done in 90 days"
  - "managing vs leading"
  - "ideas and action"

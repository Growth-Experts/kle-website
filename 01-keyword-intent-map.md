# KLE Keyword Intent Map (OC/LA Focus)

Primary conversion goal: book a consultation call.
Primary geo focus: Orange County and Los Angeles.

## Guardrails To Prevent Cannibalisation

- `services` pages target commercial intent by offer type.
- `locations` pages target geo-modified intent and route to relevant services.
- `approach`/`framework` pages target method education intent, not service terms.
- `results`/`case-studies` pages target proof and trust intent.
